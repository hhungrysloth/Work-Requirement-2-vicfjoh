About me

My name is Victoria, but I prefer to be called Vic.

I use most of my time on engagement within different causes like mental health, social justice, climate change and animal rights. In my home there are more animals than humans, and I'm very happy that I get to create a safe environment for all my adoptive "children".

Photography

One of my favorite things to do is photography. There is something magical that happens when you capture a moment to be kept forever.

I mostly focus on taking pictures at protests and other events that align with my values and the causes I'm engaged in.

Webapp development and design

I decided that I wanted to go back to studying because of my interest in development and design. I love to make art and do creative work, and my hope is to become a good asset in this field and use my new knowledge to create new apps.

Even though I have some experience in coding, it's been a while and I feel like I'm starting completely from scratch. My experience is also mostly in backend development and I have not focused a lot on frontend before, even if my biggest interest in the field is frontend and design.
